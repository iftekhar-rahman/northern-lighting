About us

NORTHERN LIGHTING is focused on providing LED Lighting solutions for corporate clients. Northern Lighting has done LED lighting upgrades for many clients such as REMAX, BMW, BlackBerry, Pepsi, GFS, Prospera, NAPA Auto and many other international brands. We help organizations operate more efficiently and save on energy consumption. We supply only premium quality LED lighting solutions with standard 5 year warranty.
